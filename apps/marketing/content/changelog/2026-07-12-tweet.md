What we shipped this week @ Superset 🛳️

1. Rich input for the terminal ⌨️

CLI agents are where the work happens, but a raw TTY line is a terrible place to write a real prompt: no multiline editing, no mentions, and pasting can execute line-by-line.

So we brought the workspace chat composer into the terminal. Press ⌘I over any terminal pane and you get a real editor: Shift+Enter newlines, @file mentions, drafts that survive tab switches. Your prompt lands in the TUI as one clean block. The terminal stays the terminal; the input just stops fighting you.

2. Terminal scrolling at Ghostty speed 🖱️

Claude Code transcripts used to scroll at a third of native speed in Superset. We hunted it down to how agent TUIs tune themselves per terminal, fixed the identity we advertise, and now scrolling matches Ghostty.

3. Mistral Vibe 🤝

Mistral's Vibe CLI is now a first-class Superset agent, contributed by the Mistral team themselves: its own icon in every picker, a model selector, working indicator, completion chime. We also added GPT-5.6 Sol, Terra, and Luna to the Codex picker. Bring whatever agent you like; Superset is where they all run.

Also this week: wake offline hosts with a configurable wake command, remote hosts on every plan (relay no longer needs a paid subscription), a Sydney relay region, and Linear project/cycle filters for Tasks.

As always, tons of bug fixes and improvements, thanks to all our users in open beta for V2 helping us make the product better everyday <3
